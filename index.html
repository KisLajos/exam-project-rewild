About us...
We are a group of people educating citizens of Aarhus on sustainability and making the city more biodiverse through projects and workshops.

This is us...

We have said “biodiversity” many times now but what does it even mean?
To put it simply, the more species of insects, plants, animals, fungi and bacteria there are in Aarhus; the more biodiverse it is.

Why do we need biodiversity?
Biodiversity provides ecosystems that give us oxygen, clean air and water, pollination of plants, pest control, wastewater treatment and many ecosystem services.
All these are important in making our lives better.

Become a part of the bottom-up movement for a greener and more biodiverse city.

With our projects and workshops for the citizens of Aarhus, we educate them to make the city greener. We have a great time with great people too!
